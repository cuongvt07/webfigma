Description

The Brooks Ghost 15 is a neutral running shoe designed for runners seeking a smooth, cushioned ride. Its DNA LOFT v2 midsole offers a softer, lighter feel with excellent shock absorption, ideal for long-distance or daily runs. The shoe features engineered mesh uppers for enhanced breathability and a snug fit, while the Segmented Crash Pad ensures smooth transitions from heel to toe. Known for its versatility, the Ghost 15 is suitable for both beginners and experienced runners.

Key Features:

  • Midsole: DNA LOFT v2 cushioning for a softer feel.
  • Upper: Engineered mesh for breathability and comfort.
  • Outsole: Durable rubber with segmented crash pads for smooth landings.
  • Fit: True to size with medium arch support.
  • Best For: Daily training, road running, and long-distance races.

This shoe offers a balanced combination of comfort, durability, and performance, making it a popular choice for runners at all levels.
